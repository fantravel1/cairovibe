Nola Cupcakes

Cairo's cupcake queens. Nola has mastered the art of the perfect cupcake and wrapped it in some of the city's most photogenic interiors. Come for the sweets, stay for the atmosphere.

View Menu Locations

The Vibe

Nola understood early that a cafe is about more than just food. Each location is designed with obsessive attention to detail - think pastel colors, Instagram-perfect corners, and interiors that make you want to photograph everything. The cupcakes just happen to taste as good as they look.

The crowd is young, female-leaning, and always taking photos. But don't let that fool you - the baking is serious. Every cupcake is made fresh, the coffee is proper, and the seasonal specials are genuinely creative.
