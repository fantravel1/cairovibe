Beano's

★ 4.5 $$ 📍 Multiple Locations ☕ Specialty Coffee
Single Origin Artisan Bakes Pour Over Work-Friendly

Cairo's answer to the global specialty coffee movement. Beano's takes coffee seriously - single-origin beans roasted in-house, skilled baristas who care about extraction, and an environment that respects both the craft and the customer. If you know your Chemex from your V60, this is your place.

Coffee Menu Find Location

The Craft

Beano's was born from frustration with Cairo's coffee status quo. The founders, serious coffee nerds, couldn't find the quality they craved, so they built it themselves. They import green beans from Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and beyond, roasting them in small batches at their Cairo facility.

The result is coffee that can compete with specialty shops anywhere in the world. The baristas are trained to international standards, and the equipment - La Marzocco machines, precision grinders, pour-over bars - reflects the investment. This isn't pretentious coffee culture; it's simply good coffee done right.

Pro Tips

  • Ask the baristas about the current single-origin options
  • Pour-over takes 5-7 minutes - worth the wait
  • Buy beans to take home - roast dates are printed
  • The croissants sell out by noon on weekends
  • WiFi is good, outlets are plentiful - work-friendly
  • They do coffee workshops if you want to learn
