The Chain

Before Cilantro, Cairo didn't have modern cafes in the Western sense. There were ahwas (traditional coffeehouses), hotel lobbies, and not much in between. Cilantro changed that, creating spaces where you could work on a laptop, hold a meeting, or just enjoy a proper coffee in comfortable surroundings.

With 50+ locations across Cairo, there's almost always a Cilantro nearby. Quality is consistent - you know what you're getting. It's not specialty coffee, but it's reliably good, and the service is fast.
