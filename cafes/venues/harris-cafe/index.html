Harris Cafe

★ 4.2 $ 📍 Downtown ☕ Traditional
Historic Budget Breakfast Local Favorite

A Downtown institution. Harris Cafe has anchored Talaat Harb Street for generations, serving tea, Turkish coffee, and Egyptian breakfast to artists, writers, journalists, and everyone else who makes Downtown tick. The atmosphere is pure Cairo.

The Experience

Harris isn't about specialty coffee or artisanal anything. It's about tradition. The brass trays, the waiters in their distinctive uniforms, the sound of backgammon pieces and animated conversation. Come for the atmosphere as much as the food - this is living Cairo history.

Pro Tips

  • Morning and late afternoon are prime people-watching hours
  • Grab a sidewalk table for the full experience
  • Cash only - and prices are delightfully old-school
  • Combine with a walk through Downtown's Art Deco architecture
