Cafe Greco

★ 4.3 $$ 📍 Zamalek ☕ Italian Style
Espresso Pastries European Brunch

A slice of Rome in Zamalek. Cafe Greco has been serving proper Italian espresso and fresh pastries for decades. The marble-topped bar, the vintage Gaggia machine, the morning crowd of regulars - it's as close to an authentic Italian cafe experience as you'll find in Cairo.

The Coffee

Coffee here is treated with respect. Espresso comes in proper cups, made by baristas who understand extraction. The cappuccino follows Italian rules - small, strong, and only acceptable before noon if you want to fit in. They also do an excellent affogato for an afternoon treat.

Pro Tips

  • Morning is the best time - join the espresso-at-the-bar crowd
  • The cornetti are baked fresh daily
  • Outdoor seats are perfect for people-watching
  • Cash is preferred for small orders
