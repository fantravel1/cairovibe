Event Guide

Events in Cairo

CairoVibe provides curated event listings. Other good sources include Cairo 360, Scoop Empire, and venue social media pages (Instagram is particularly active). Facebook Events is still widely used in Egypt. For concerts and big events, check El Sawy Culturewheel, Cairo Opera House, and major venue pages.

Many events can be paid at the door. For popular concerts and festivals, buy tickets in advance through platforms like Tazkartak or directly from venues. Some events require reservations via WhatsApp or social media. Credit cards are accepted at larger venues; smaller events are often cash-only.

Cairo Jazz Club hosts live music multiple times weekly. El Sawy Culturewheel has daily events. Many rooftops have weekly sunset sessions on Fridays. Downtown has a thriving underground music scene with regular events at various venues. Art galleries in Zamalek have rotating exhibitions and opening nights.
