Harry's Pub

★ 4.1 $$$ 📍 Marriott Zamalek 🏛 Historic
Hotel Bar Classic Beer Institution

A Cairo institution since the 1980s. Harry's Pub occupies a cozy corner of the historic Marriott Hotel, offering classic pub vibes within the walls of a 19th-century palace. Generations of expats and Cairenes have made this their local.
