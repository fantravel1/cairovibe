La Bodega

★ 4.3 $$$ 📍 Zamalek 🍷 Wine Bar
Tapas Wine Spanish Date Night

A taste of Spain in Zamalek. La Bodega captures the essence of Spanish wine bar culture - small plates meant for sharing, wines by the glass, and an atmosphere that encourages lingering conversation. Warm, inviting, and consistently excellent.

The Experience

La Bodega's terracotta walls, wooden barrels, and strings of garlic create an atmosphere straight from Barcelona. The concept is simple: order a few tapas, a bottle (or carafe) of wine, and let the evening unfold naturally. It's social dining at its best.
