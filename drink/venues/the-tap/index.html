The Tap

★ 4.4 $$ 📍 Maadi & Mohandessin 🍺 Craft Beer
Craft Beer Pub Food Sports Expat Friendly

Cairo's craft beer revolution started here. The Tap brought quality Egyptian-brewed craft beers to a city that was starving for them, wrapped in a welcoming pub atmosphere that feels like a neighborhood local.

View Beers Locations

The Bar

Before The Tap, Cairo's beer options were limited to Stella (the Egyptian one, not the Belgian) and a handful of imports. The Tap changed that by partnering with local craft breweries to offer a rotating selection of real ales, IPAs, stouts, and lagers - all brewed in Egypt.

The vibe is casual pub: wooden interiors, sports on the screens, good bar food, and a crowd that mixes expats, Egyptians, and anyone who appreciates a properly poured pint. It's the kind of place where you come for one beer and stay for five.

Pro Tips

  • Thursday nights are the busiest - arrive early for a seat
  • They show major sports events - check Facebook for schedules
  • The flight of 4 tasters is the best way to try the range
  • Kitchen closes before the bar - order food early
  • Maadi location has better parking
