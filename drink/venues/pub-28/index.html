Pub 28

★ 4.2 $$ 📍 Zamalek 🍺 Pub
Beer Pub Grub Sports Casual

The neighborhood pub Zamalek deserves. Pub 28 is unpretentious, friendly, and reliable. Cold beer, decent food, screens for sports - everything you need for a casual night out without the fuss of Cairo's flashier venues.

The Vibe

Dark wood, dim lighting, football scarves on the walls - Pub 28 nails the British pub aesthetic. The crowd is mixed: expats, locals, young professionals, and the occasional tourist. It's the kind of place where everyone feels welcome.
