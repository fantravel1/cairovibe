Live Music Venue

After Eight

★ 4.3 $$ 📍 Downtown 🎸 Live Music
Live Music Rock Alternative Underground

Downtown's living room for Cairo's alternative music scene. After Eight has been hosting live bands since the 1990s, providing a platform for local rock, jazz, and experimental musicians. It's gritty, authentic, and irreplaceable.

The Scene

After Eight isn't polished - that's the point. The sound system is functional, the seating is random, and the decor is decades of band posters. But for Cairo's music lovers, it's sacred ground. This is where Egyptian rock was born, where jazz musicians jam, where new acts get their start.
