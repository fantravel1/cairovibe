Saigon

★ 4.5 $$$ 📍 Zamalek 🍸 Asian-Inspired
Cocktails Asian Fusion Late Night Date Night

Cairo's love letter to Southeast Asian bar culture. Saigon brings the moody, seductive vibe of Bangkok and Ho Chi Minh City speakeasies to Zamalek, with creative cocktails that incorporate Asian ingredients and small plates that pack serious flavor.

Drink Menu Food Menu

The Vibe

Walk down the unmarked stairs off a Zamalek side street and you enter another world. Saigon is all dark wood, red lighting, and ceiling fans turning slowly overhead. The space evokes colonial-era Indochina - ceiling fans, rattan, vintage posters, and a long wooden bar where the bartenders work their magic.

The crowd is Cairo's creative class - artists, designers, the fashionable and culturally curious. The music shifts from chill to upbeat as the night progresses. Come early for intimate conversation, stay late when it transforms into a party.

Pro Tips

  • No sign outside - look for the red lantern above a nondescript door
  • Arrive before 10 PM to get a seat without waiting
  • The bar seats are best for watching the cocktail show
  • Ask the bartenders for recommendations - they know their stuff
  • Thursday is DJ night - comes alive after midnight
