Jazz Bar 929

★ 4.5 $$$ 📍 Heliopolis 🎷 Live Jazz
Live Music Jazz Cocktails Intimate

Where Cairo's jazz scene comes alive. This intimate Heliopolis club hosts live performances most nights, with local and visiting musicians playing in a space that feels like a 1950s New York jazz club. Sip a whiskey sour and let the music transport you.

The Music

Jazz Bar 929 is about the music first. Local jazz combos, visiting international artists, and open jam sessions create a rotating calendar of performances. The acoustics are carefully tuned, and the stage is close enough that you can practically reach out and touch the musicians.
