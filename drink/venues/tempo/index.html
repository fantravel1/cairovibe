Wine Bar

Tempo

★ 4.4 $$$ 📍 Maadi 🍷 Wine Focus
Wine Bar Bistro French Garden

Maadi's grown-up drinking spot. Tempo combines an impressive wine cellar with French bistro cooking in a setting that feels like it could be in Paris. This is where Maadi's expat community and Cairo's oenophiles come for sophisticated evenings.

Wine List Food Menu

The Space

Tempo occupies a charming Maadi villa, with the main bar and dining room inside and a lovely garden terrace outside. The interior has the feel of a European wine bar - exposed brick, wooden shelves lined with bottles, soft lighting from vintage fixtures. The garden is strung with fairy lights and perfect for Cairo's cooler months.

The atmosphere is relaxed but refined. Conversations flow easily, the music is background-appropriate jazz and French classics, and the staff actually know their wines. It's the kind of place where a glass turns into a bottle and one course becomes three.

The Wine List

One of Cairo's most thoughtfully curated wine programs. The list spans the classic regions - France, Italy, Spain - with interesting selections from Lebanon, South Africa, and Argentina. Around 20 wines available by the glass, with rotating specials.

Pro Tips

  • The garden is the place to be - book ahead on weekends
  • Wednesday wine tastings feature different regions each week
  • The cheese board and a good red make a perfect evening
  • Ask sommelier recommendations - they're genuinely knowledgeable
  • Happy hour (5-7 PM) has 25% off selected wines by glass
