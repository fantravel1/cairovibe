Le Pacha 1901

★ 4.3 $$$ 📍 Zamalek 🚢 On the Nile
Nile Views Multiple Venues Historic Nightlife

A Cairo institution permanently moored at Zamalek's northern tip. Le Pacha 1901 is an entire entertainment complex on the Nile - restaurants, bars, a club, and some of the best river views in the city. One boat, endless possibilities.

Explore Venues Get Directions

The Boat

Le Pacha 1901 isn't just a bar - it's a floating city of nightlife. This permanently moored vessel at Zamalek's tip has been a Cairo institution for decades, hosting everything from casual drinks to legendary late nights. The boat houses multiple distinct venues across several decks, each with its own vibe.

The location is unbeatable: where the Nile splits around Gezira Island, with views of both banks and the bridges lit up at night. On the upper decks, you feel like you're floating in the middle of Cairo.

The Venues

L'Asiatique

Upscale Pan-Asian restaurant on the upper deck. Sushi, Thai, Chinese - with panoramic Nile views.

When to Go

  • Sunset (6-8 PM): Le Deck for golden hour drinks
  • Dinner (8-11 PM): L'Asiatique for upscale dining
  • Late Night (11 PM+): Papas Lounge for dancing
  • Sports Events: Pier 88 for match screenings

Pro Tips

  • Le Deck is the sweet spot - great views, good prices, chill vibe
  • Weekends get crowded - arrive early for the best spots
  • The boat doesn't move - it's permanently moored
  • Dress code is smart casual for restaurants, relaxed for bars
  • Easy Uber/Careem pickup from the main road
