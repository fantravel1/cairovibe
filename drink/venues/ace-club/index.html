ACE Club

★ 4.4 $$$$ 📍 Fairmont Nile City 🌃 Rooftop
Rooftop Cocktails City Views Upscale

Cairo from above. ACE Club perches atop the Fairmont Nile City, offering 360-degree views of the city, river, and pyramids in the distance. The cocktails are expertly crafted, the vibe is sophisticated, and the sunsets are unforgettable.

The View

Few venues in Cairo can match ACE's panorama. On clear days, you can see the Pyramids of Giza in the distance. At night, the city lights spread out beneath you. It's the kind of view that makes you fall in love with Cairo all over again.
