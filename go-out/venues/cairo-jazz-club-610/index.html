Cairo Jazz Club 610

★ 4.4 $$$ 📍 Sheikh Zayed 🎵 Live Music
Live Music Art Culture CJC Family

The Cairo Jazz Club legacy continues westward. CJC 610 brings the original's commitment to live music and cultural programming to Sheikh Zayed in a purpose-built venue with expanded space for art exhibitions, performances, and community events.

The Venue

CJC 610 takes everything the original Cairo Jazz Club stood for and scales it up. The main performance space hosts bands and DJs, while dedicated gallery spaces showcase local and international artists. There's an outdoor area for warm evenings and a restaurant serving quality food throughout the night.

Pro Tips

  • Check the schedule - programming varies nightly
  • Reserve for dinner to guarantee entry on busy nights
  • The gallery exhibitions are free to browse
  • Parking is easier than at the Agouza location
  • Combines well with dinner at nearby Sheikh Zayed restaurants
