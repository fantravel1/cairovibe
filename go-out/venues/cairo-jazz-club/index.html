Cairo Jazz Club

★ 4.5 $$ 📍 Agouza 🎵 Live Music Nightly
Live Music Jazz Funk Late Night

The beating heart of Cairo's live music scene. CJC has been championing local and international artists for decades, creating a space where jazz, funk, rock, and electronic all find a home. This is where Cairo comes to discover new sounds.

The Venue

Cairo Jazz Club is more than a bar - it's an institution. For over two decades, CJC has been the launchpad for Egyptian musical talent and a beacon for international touring artists. The intimate space creates a connection between performers and audience that larger venues can't match.

The crowd is eclectic - musicians, artists, expats, students, and anyone who loves live music. The vibe is unpretentious and welcoming. You might see a jazz trio one night, an Afrobeat band the next, and a DJ set after midnight.

Pro Tips

  • Check their Instagram for tonight's lineup - it changes constantly
  • Arrive by 10 PM on weekends for a good spot
  • The front area near the stage gets packed - back bar is more chill
  • No cover most nights, some special events charge EGP 100-200
  • Thursday and Friday are the biggest nights
  • They also have a second venue - CJC 610 - with different programming
