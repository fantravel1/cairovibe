The Loft

★ 4.2 $$$ 📍 New Cairo 🎶 Electronic
Electronic International DJs Late Night Premium

Cairo's temple of electronic music. The Loft brings world-class production values and international DJ bookings to a purpose-built venue. If you're serious about house, techno, or anything that moves a dancefloor, this is where you belong.

The Venue

The Loft was built for electronic music. The sound system is world-class, the lighting rig rivals international clubs, and the industrial-chic design creates an atmosphere of controlled intensity. Two rooms offer different vibes - the main room for headliners, and a second space for deeper sounds.

What to Expect

Entry (standard nights)EGP 300-500
Entry (international events)EGP 800-1,500
DrinksEGP 150-250
Table ServiceEGP 5,000+

Pro Tips

  • Check social media for lineup announcements
  • Things don't really get going until after midnight
  • Strict door policy - dress well, come with mixed groups
  • International events sell out - buy tickets early
  • Earplugs are your friend
