The Club

OPIA raised the bar for Cairo nightlife when it opened. The space is designed with serious clubbers in mind - a Funktion-One sound system that you feel in your chest, lighting that transforms the room, and a layout that keeps energy flowing from the main floor to the terraces.

The crowd is Cairo's young elite - models, entrepreneurs, socialites, and anyone who takes their nights out seriously. The music policy leans electronic - house, techno, and the occasional international headliner who knows Egypt's party scene is worth the trip.

Know Before You Go

  • Dress code: Strictly enforced. Smart/trendy - no sportswear, no sandals
  • Entry: Guest list recommended, couples prioritized
  • Age: 21+ strictly enforced with ID
  • Arrive: Midnight-1 AM is prime time
  • Closes: Around 4-5 AM
  • Getting home: Uber/Careem - have the app ready
