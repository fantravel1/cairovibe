Kazoku

★ 4.5 $$$ 📍 New Cairo 🍣 Japanese
Sushi Ramen Omakase Izakaya

Cairo's most authentic Japanese experience. Kazoku (meaning "family" in Japanese) brings the real deal - fresh fish flown in regularly, a sushi counter where you can watch masters work, and ramen that would hold its own in Tokyo. This is Japanese food taken seriously.

View Menu Reserve

The Space

Kazoku occupies a sleek, minimalist space that evokes contemporary Tokyo. The main dining room is bright and airy, but the real action is at the sushi counter - eight seats where you can watch the itamae (sushi chef) work their magic. The private tatami room seats up to six for intimate dinners.

The attention to detail extends to everything - the ceramics are imported from Japan, the sake list is extensive and thoughtfully curated, and the service walks the line between attentive and unobtrusive that Japanese hospitality demands.

The Sake Selection

One of Cairo's best sake lists, ranging from approachable junmai to premium daiginjo. The staff can guide you to the right pairing - don't be afraid to ask. They also do excellent Japanese whisky and shochu.

Pro Tips

  • Book the sushi counter for omakase - it's a different experience
  • Fish deliveries are twice weekly - ask what's freshest
  • The lunch sets are excellent value
  • Try the seasonal specials - they change with Japanese seasons
  • Sake flights let you explore different styles
