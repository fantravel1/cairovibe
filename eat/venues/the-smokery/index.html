The Smokery

★ 4.5 $$ 📍 Maadi 🍖 Smoked Meats
BBQ Brisket Ribs Casual

Cairo's temple of American BBQ. The Smokery does low-and-slow smoking properly - 14-hour brisket, fall-off-the-bone ribs, and homemade sauces. If you're craving authentic Texas-style barbecue, this is your spot.

The Smoke

Everything here is smoked on-site using imported wood chips. The brisket goes in the smoker at night and comes out the next afternoon. The result is tender, smoky, perfectly seasoned meat that would hold its own in Austin.
