OVIO

★ 4.4 $$$ 📍 New Cairo 🍝 Italian
Pasta Pizza Contemporary Family-Friendly

New Cairo's Italian darling. OVIO combines the warmth of a neighborhood trattoria with the sophistication of a contemporary restaurant. Fresh pasta made daily, wood-fired pizzas, and a genuinely Italian approach to hospitality.

The Kitchen

Everything at OVIO is made with intention. Pasta is rolled fresh each morning, pizzas emerge blistered from the wood-burning oven, and the antipasti display showcases seasonal Italian produce. The open kitchen lets you watch the chefs work their magic.
