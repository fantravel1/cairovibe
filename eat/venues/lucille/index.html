Lucille's

★ 4.3 $$ 📍 Zamalek 🍳 All-Day Breakfast
Brunch Burgers Milkshakes Casual

Zamalek's slice of Americana. Lucille's channels classic diner energy - red booths, checkered floors, and a menu of comfort food favorites. Whether it's pancakes at noon or a burger at midnight, they've got you covered.

The Vibe

Walk into Lucille's and you're transported to a 1950s American roadside diner. The design is fun without being cheesy - all neon, chrome, and vinyl. The crowd is young, happy, and usually nursing a milkshake or cocktail alongside their food.
