Bird Cage

★ 4.5 $$$ 📍 Garden City 🏨 Semiramis Hotel
Thai Asian Hotel Restaurant Award-Winning

Cairo's most authentic Thai experience. Bird Cage has been serving genuine Thai flavors for decades, with recipes and techniques straight from Bangkok. The exotic interior transports you across continents.

The Experience

Step into Bird Cage and you're transported to Thailand. Ornate birdcages hang from the ceiling, Thai silks drape the walls, and the aromas of lemongrass and galangal fill the air. Thai chefs prepare each dish with authentic ingredients, many flown in specially.
