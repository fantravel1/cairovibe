Tabla Luna

★ 4.7 $$$$ 📍 Zamalek 🍽 Mediterranean
Fine Dining Date Night Wine List Seasonal

When Cairo does fine dining right, it looks like Tabla Luna. Mediterranean foundations with subtle Middle Eastern touches, executed with precision in one of Zamalek's most elegant spaces. This is where you come for celebrations, anniversaries, or when you want to impress.

View Menu Reserve

The Experience

Tabla Luna occupies a beautifully restored villa that sets the tone before you've even tasted the food. The interior design strikes a balance between contemporary elegance and warmth - this is fine dining without stuffiness. The garden terrace, open in cooler months, is particularly romantic.

The kitchen is led by a chef who trained in Europe but cooks with a deep understanding of local ingredients and traditions. The result is Mediterranean cuisine that feels both familiar and surprising - classic techniques applied to regional produce, with Middle Eastern flavors woven in subtly.

The Wine List

One of Cairo's most impressive wine programs. The sommelier has curated a list that spans Old World classics and New World discoveries, with several available by the glass. Don't miss the Lebanese and Egyptian selections - they're surprisingly good.

Pro Tips

  • Reservations essential, especially weekends
  • Ask for the garden terrace in spring/autumn
  • The tasting menu is the best way to experience the kitchen
  • Dress code is smart casual - no shorts or sandals
  • They accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice
