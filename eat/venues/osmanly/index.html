Osmanly

★ 4.7 $$$$ 📍 Garden City 🏨 Kempinski Hotel
Turkish Ottoman Nile Views Fine Dining

Ottoman grandeur meets Nile views. Osmanly resurrects the sophisticated cuisine of the Ottoman court in a setting of breathtaking elegance. This is special occasion dining at its finest.

The Experience

Perched atop the Kempinski Nile Hotel, Osmanly commands sweeping views of the river and city. The interior evokes Istanbul's finest, with intricate tilework, rich fabrics, and soft lighting. The kitchen revives forgotten Ottoman recipes with modern refinement.
