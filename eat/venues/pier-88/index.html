Pier 88

★ 4.2 $$ 📍 Zamalek (Le Pacha) 🏈 Sports Bar
Burgers Sports Beer Casual

Cairo's go-to spot for game day. Pier 88 brings American sports bar culture to the Nile, with solid burgers, crispy wings, cold beers, and screens showing every major match. Casual, fun, and always buzzing.

The Vibe

Located aboard the Le Pacha 1901 boat, Pier 88 is all exposed brick, neon signs, and sports memorabilia. Multiple big screens mean you'll never miss a game. The crowd is a mix of expats, sports fans, and groups looking for a fun, casual night.
