Andrea

★ 4.5 $$ 📍 Giza 📅 Since 1978
Grilled Chicken Garden Dining Egyptian Family

Andrea is where Cairo goes for the perfect grilled chicken. Set in sprawling gardens along the Mariouteya Canal, this institution has been serving up Egyptian classics since 1978. It's not just a restaurant - it's a Cairo rite of passage.

View Menu Get Directions

The Legend

Every Cairene has an Andrea story. First dates, family gatherings, post-pyramid lunches - this garden restaurant has hosted them all. The setting is magical: sprawling outdoor terraces under mature trees, with the canal glinting in the background and smoke rising from the grills.

The star is the chicken - whole birds marinated in a secret blend of spices and grilled over open flames until the skin is crispy and the meat falls off the bone. It's simple, it's perfect, and it's been unchanged for over 45 years.

The Experience

Andrea is best experienced on a warm evening. Grab a garden table, order a whole chicken with tahini and pickles, and settle in. The pace is leisurely - this isn't a quick meal. Watch families celebrate, couples share plates, and kids run between the trees while the grill masters work their magic.

Pro Tips

  • The original Giza location (Mariouteya) is the best - other branches don't compare
  • Weekend lunches are packed - go on a weekday or arrive by noon
  • The tahini here is legendary - order extra
  • Perfect post-Pyramids lunch (15 min drive)
  • No alcohol, but the fresh juices are excellent
  • They don't take reservations - just show up
