Taboula

★ 4.6 $$$ 📍 Zamalek
Lebanese Mezze Garden Date Night

The gold standard for Lebanese cuisine in Cairo. Taboula elevates the familiar mezze-and-grills format into something special, with meticulous preparation, beautiful presentation, and a setting that transports you to Beirut's finest dining rooms.

The Experience

Set in a gorgeous restored villa with a magical garden terrace, Taboula gets everything right. The mezze are impeccably fresh, the grilled meats are perfectly charred, and the arak flows freely. Service is polished without being stiff.
