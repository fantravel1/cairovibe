El Dahan

★ 4.6 $$ 📍 Giza 🔥 Since 1952
Kofta Kebab Institution Local Favorite

When Egyptians argue about the best kofta in Cairo, El Dahan always enters the conversation. Three generations of one family have perfected the art of grilled meat here, earning a devoted following that spans decades. This is Egyptian comfort food at its finest.

The Legend

El Dahan opened in 1952 and hasn't changed the formula since. The secret is in the meat blend, the charcoal technique, and recipes passed down through the family. Presidents, celebrities, and ordinary Cairenes alike make the pilgrimage to Haram Road for this kofta.

What to Order

Kofta (per kilo)EGP 450
Kebab (per kilo)EGP 500
Mixed GrillEGP 280
Reyash (lamb chops)EGP 380
Tahina & SaladsEGP 30-50

Pro Tips

  • Order by weight - half kilo feeds one hungry person
  • The kofta is the star, but kebab is excellent too
  • Load up on fresh bread and tahina
  • Combine with a Pyramids visit - perfect post-tourist meal
  • They don't serve alcohol
