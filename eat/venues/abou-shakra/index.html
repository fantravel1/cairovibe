Abou Shakra

★ 4.5 $$ 📍 Garden City 📅 Since 1947
Grilled Meats Egyptian Historic Family

Some restaurants become legends. Abou Shakra has been serving Cairo's finest grilled meats since 1947 - that's nearly 80 years of perfecting kofta, kebab, and lamb. If you eat meat in Cairo, you eat here.

View Menu Get Directions

The Legend

Abou Shakra started as a small grill in 1947 and became an institution through one simple principle: serve the best meat in Cairo. Three generations later, the quality hasn't wavered. The kofta is still hand-mixed to a secret recipe, the kebab is still cut from prime cuts, and the lamb still falls off the bone.

Presidents, celebrities, and everyday Cairenes have all made pilgrimages here. The original Garden City location remains the flagship, though branches have spread across the city. This is Egyptian comfort food at its finest - no fusion, no trends, just perfect execution of classics.

What to Order

First-timers: get the Mixed Grill. It gives you everything Abou Shakra does best on one plate. The kofta is the star - that secret spice blend is worth the trip alone. Add tahini, baba ganoush, and rice with nuts on the side.

For groups: order the lamb shoulder (call ahead) and share. It's a showstopper that feeds 2-3 hungry people.

Pro Tips

  • Go to the Garden City original for the authentic experience
  • Lunch is less crowded than dinner
  • Everything comes with unlimited fresh bread - pace yourself
  • No alcohol served - it's a family restaurant
  • Vegetarians: the mezze are good but this is a meat destination
  • They do takeaway if you want to picnic by the Nile
