Zooba

The restaurant that made Egyptian street food cool again. Zooba takes the classics - taameya, koshari, hawawshi, feteer - and elevates them with premium ingredients and modern presentation. No-frills vibes, incredible flavor.

Call to Reserve View Highlights

What Makes It Special

Zooba started as a single shop with a simple mission: prove that Egyptian street food deserves respect. Founders Chris Khalifa and Mostafa Elrefaey stripped away the stigma of "street food" and focused on quality - farm-fresh vegetables, house-made sauces, and traditional recipes done right.

The result? A casual, counter-service restaurant that's become a Cairo institution. You'll find everyone here - students, businesspeople, families, celebrities - all queuing for the same thing: taameya that shatters when you bite it, koshari with the perfect balance of carbs and crunch, and the best hawawshi in the city.

The Vibe

Zooba is deliberately casual - colorful tiles, communal seating, open kitchen. It's designed for quick meals and takeaway, not lingering. The energy is fast and friendly, with staff who genuinely know and love the food. Come hungry, leave happy.

Pro Tips

  • Order the "Zooba Breakfast" combo for the full experience
  • Get the koshari with extra crispy onions and a side of their special hot sauce
  • Taameya is best eaten immediately - don't let it get cold
  • The Maadi branch has more seating than Zamalek
  • They deliver via most apps, but dine-in is the way to go
