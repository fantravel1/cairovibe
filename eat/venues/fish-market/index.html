Fish Market

★ 4.4 $$$ 📍 Heliopolis 🐟 Interactive
Seafood Fresh Catch Hotel Dining Family

Cairo's most interactive seafood experience. Walk up to the ice display, point to your fish, choose how you want it cooked, and wait for magic. Fish Market has been Cairo's seafood destination for over two decades.

The Experience

The centerpiece is the dramatic ice display showcasing the day's catch - everything from local Mediterranean fish to imported seafood. You select your fish (priced by weight), choose your cooking style, and add your sides. It's engaging, transparent, and guarantees freshness.

How It Works

  1. Browse the ice display with staff guidance
  2. Select your fish (priced per kg)
  3. Choose: grilled, fried, baked, or sayadeya style
  4. Add appetizers and sides from the menu
  5. Enjoy perfectly cooked seafood
