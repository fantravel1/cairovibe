Pro Tips

Late Night Survival Guide

🚗 Getting Around

Uber and Careem work 24/7. Taxis are abundant but negotiate prices after midnight. Metro closes around midnight.

💰 Cash vs Card

Street food is cash only. Bars and restaurants take cards. ATMs are everywhere. Keep small bills for street vendors.

🛡 Safety

Cairo is generally safe late night. Stick to busy areas, use rideshare apps, and avoid walking alone in unfamiliar neighborhoods.

🎉 Best Nights

Thursday and Friday are the big nights. Saturday is active but mellower. Weeknights are quieter but venues stay open.
