Live Music Questions

The best live music venues in Cairo include Cairo Jazz Club in Agouza for indie and jazz, El Sawy Culture Wheel for diverse performances, Room Art Space for alternative acts, and various hotel bars for jazz and lounge music.

Thursday through Saturday are the best nights for live music. Most venues have their headlining acts on Fridays, while Thursdays often feature indie and up-and-coming artists.
