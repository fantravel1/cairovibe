Budget Cairo

Cairo is one of the world's most affordable major cities. Here's how to eat like a king, drink well, and experience everything without emptying your wallet.

💡 Budget Quick Guide

Street Food Meal

EGP 30-60 (~$1-2)

Local Beer

EGP 50-80 (~$1.50-2.50)

Metro Ride

EGP 8-12 (~$0.30)

Uber (5km)

EGP 40-70 (~$1.50-2)

Cheap Eats That Deliver

Egyptian Street Food

Koshari Abou Tarek

★ 4.5 $ Downtown

Cairo's most famous koshari. A mountain of carbs for ~EGP 40.

Ful & Taameya

Zooba

★ 4.6 $ Multiple

Elevated street food. Full meal for under EGP 100.

Shawarma

Arabiata

★ 4.3 $ Multiple

Reliable shawarma chain. Quick, cheap, satisfying.

Grilled Meats

Kebdet El Prince

★ 4.4 $ Imbaba

Legendary liver sandwiches. Worth the trip.

Drinks

Affordable Bars & Cafes

Dive Bar

Odeon Palace Hotel Bar

★ 4.0 $ Downtown

Cairo's legendary rooftop dive. Cheap beers, retro vibes, unforgettable atmosphere.

Traditional Ahwa

El Fishawi

★ 4.2 $ Khan El Khalili

200-year-old coffeehouse. Tea, shisha, and history for pocket change.

Pub

Deals

No-frills expat pub. Happy hour specials, sports on TV.

Free & Cheap

Activities That Won't Break the Bank

🚶 Free

  • Walk the Nile Corniche at sunset
  • Explore Islamic Cairo's streets
  • Window shop in Khan El Khalili
  • Walk around Zamalek
  • Watch the pyramids from the road

🎫 Under EGP 100

  • Felucca ride (negotiate!)
  • Cairo Tower observation deck
  • Live music at El Sawy
  • Metro tour of the city
  • Local cinema

🏛 Museum Deals

  • Egyptian Museum - EGP 200 (worth it)
  • Coptic Museum - EGP 100
  • Islamic Art Museum - EGP 100
  • Students get 50% off
