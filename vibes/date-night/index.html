Date Night Cairo

From first dates to anniversaries, Cairo delivers romance like few cities can. Candlelit dinners on the Nile, rooftop cocktails under the stars, and experiences you'll remember forever.

💡 Date Night Pro Tips

Reserve Early

Book Nile-view and rooftop tables at least 3 days ahead, especially for weekends.

Time It Right

Sunset reservations (6-7 PM in winter, 7-8 PM in summer) are prime time.

Dress Up

Upscale venues expect smart casual at minimum. When in doubt, overdress.

Plan the After

Don't end at dinner - add drinks, a walk, or dessert at a different spot.
