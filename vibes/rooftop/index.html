Rooftop FAQs

The best rooftop bars in Cairo include Crimson Bar & Grill in Zamalek for Nile views, 9 Pyramids Lounge in Giza for pyramid views, The Roof at Kempinski for Garden City elegance, and O Bar at the Four Seasons for upscale cocktails. Each offers stunning views and quality drinks.

Most Cairo rooftop bars have a smart casual dress code. For men, nice jeans or chinos with a collared shirt work well. Women can wear dresses, nice tops with pants, or stylish jumpsuits. Avoid shorts, flip-flops, sportswear, or overly casual attire.

Yes, reservations are strongly recommended for Cairo's popular rooftop bars, especially on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights. For special views like pyramid-facing tables at Giza venues, book at least a week in advance. Weekday evenings are more flexible.
