Neighborhood

6th of October City

Cairo's western satellite city - malls, universities, and suburban sprawl done Egyptian style.

Built in the desert west of Cairo, 6th of October is home to major universities, malls, and a growing food and entertainment scene. It's not walkable or charming, but it has its own energy.

The Malls

Mall of Arabia and Mall of Egypt are the big draws - shopping, cinemas, dining, and Egypt's only indoor ski slope at Mall of Egypt.

Where to Eat

Eatery Food Hall - Multiple cuisines under one roof
Mall dining - All major chains represented
Arkan Plaza - Growing restaurant cluster

Getting There

About 45 minutes from central Cairo by car (traffic dependent). Uber/Careem recommended. The Monorail is under construction.
