Neighborhood Guide

Finding Your Perfect Area

For nightlife, Zamalek and Downtown are your best bets. Zamalek offers upscale rooftop bars and lounges with stunning Nile views. Downtown has a more eclectic scene with underground venues, live music spots, and authentic local bars. New Cairo is home to the biggest clubs if you're looking for a modern party scene.

Downtown Cairo is the best area for budget travelers. You'll find affordable hostels, cheap local eats, and plenty of free attractions. Giza is another budget-friendly option with lower prices and easy access to the Pyramids. Dokki offers a good balance of affordability and safety.

Zamalek, Maadi, Garden City, and New Cairo are considered the safest areas for tourists. These neighborhoods have good security, well-lit streets, and are popular with expats. That said, Cairo is generally safe - just use common sense, especially at night.

For authentic Cairo vibes, stay in Downtown. It's the historic heart of the city with Belle Epoque architecture, bustling streets, and real local life. You'll be close to major attractions and experience the city like a local. Heliopolis is another great choice for authentic Egyptian neighborhood life.
