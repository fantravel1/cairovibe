New Cairo

🏗 Modern 💰 $$$ Upscale 🎉 Big Clubs 🛡 Very Safe

Cairo's modern face. Gleaming malls, gated compounds, and Cairo's biggest nightclubs. Where the young, affluent crowd comes to shop, eat, and party. It's suburbia Cairo-style - polished, convenient, and packed on weekends.

Explore Venues Nightclubs

The Vibe

New Cairo (officially called Tagamoa Al-Khames, or "Fifth Settlement") is Cairo's answer to American suburbia. Built from scratch starting in the 2000s, it's a city of wide roads, modern compounds, mega-malls, and the American University in Cairo campus.

The vibe is completely different from old Cairo - cleaner, more organized, more air-conditioned. It lacks the character of Downtown or Zamalek, but makes up for it with convenience and some of Cairo's biggest entertainment venues.

Getting Around

New Cairo is car-dependent. Uber and Careem are essential - there's no metro here. Traffic can be brutal during rush hour and weekends. Most venues have parking. Walking between malls is possible but not pleasant in summer heat.

Pro Tips

  • Weekend traffic to New Cairo is horrendous - leave early
  • Club dress codes are strict - no sneakers, no shorts
  • Reservations are essential for popular restaurants
  • The AUC campus has occasional public events
  • Prices here are generally higher than central Cairo
