The Cairo Foodie Trail

🍴 20+ Stops 💰 $ to $$$$ 📍 City-Wide

Cairo is a food city. From centuries-old street food traditions to contemporary fine dining, this trail takes you through the essential culinary experiences every food lover needs.

Egyptian Essentials

Before you explore, know what you're eating. These are the dishes that define Egyptian cuisine:

Koshari

Egypt's national dish - rice, pasta, lentils, chickpeas, fried onions, and spiced tomato sauce. Pure carb heaven.

Ful Medames

Slow-cooked fava beans with olive oil, lemon, and cumin. The Egyptian breakfast staple for millennia.

Taameya

Egyptian falafel made with fava beans (not chickpeas). Crispy outside, green and herby inside.

Hawawshi

Spiced minced meat stuffed in baladi bread and baked until crispy. Egyptian meat pie perfection.

Molokhia

Green soup made from jute leaves, served over rice with chicken or rabbit. The texture is unique - embrace it.

Sweet Finishes

  • Mandarine Koueider: Historic patisserie in Heliopolis. Om Ali and kunafa.
  • El Abd: Classic bakery. Basbousa and feteer meshaltet.
  • La Poire: Modern cakes and pastries. Multiple locations.
  • Street Vendors: Fresh juice carts everywhere - try sugarcane, mango, or guava.
