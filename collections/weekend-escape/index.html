48 Hour Itinerary

The Ultimate Cairo Weekend Escape

📅 2 Days 💰 $$$ 👥 Perfect for couples or friends

This is how you do Cairo in a weekend. We've mapped out 48 hours of the city's best - from lazy brunches to epic nights out, from ancient wonders to modern pleasures. Pack light, bring energy, and prepare for memories.

1 Friday: The Warm-Up

11:00 AM - Brunch

Left Bank, Zamalek

Start slow with Cairo's best brunch scene. Order the shakshuka, a flat white, and watch Zamalek wake up from the garden terrace.

Cafe Brunch
1:00 PM - Culture

Egyptian Museum

It's a classic for a reason. Spend a couple hours with the pharaohs before the Grand Egyptian Museum fully opens. Don't miss Tutankhamun's treasures.

Culture History
4:00 PM - Chill

Pool at Four Seasons

Day pass or F&B minimum - cool off at the city's most glamorous pool. Perfect pre-game for the night ahead.

Pool Luxury
7:00 PM - Sunset Drinks

Crimson Bar, Zamalek

Watch the sun set over the Nile from Cairo's best rooftop. Order the Nile Sunset cocktail - it's their signature for a reason.

Rooftop Cocktails
9:00 PM - Dinner

Sequoia

Mediterranean cuisine on the Nile. The terrace tables are magic - book ahead. Share mezze, order the grilled catch, and let the night unfold.

Dining Nile View

2 Saturday: The Main Event

7:00 AM - Adventure

Pyramids of Giza

Beat the crowds and the heat. Early morning at the Pyramids is magical - golden light, cool air, and you'll have more space to yourself.

Wonder of the World Must See
12:00 PM - Local Lunch

Andrea, Giza

Legendary grilled chicken and Egyptian classics. Sit in the garden, order the whole chicken, and embrace the post-pyramid hunger.

Egyptian Classic
3:00 PM - Explore

Downtown Cairo Walk

Wander the Belle Epoque streets, peek into vintage shops, grab coffee at Groppi, and soak in the real Cairo energy.

Walking Culture
6:00 PM - Pre-Game

Hotel Refresh

Head back, shower, rest up. Tonight's the big night - you'll want to be ready.

9:00 PM - Dinner

Zitouni, Four Seasons

The city's finest Middle Eastern cuisine. Request a Nile-view table, order the mixed grill, and celebrate being in Cairo.

Fine Dining Special Occasion
11:30 PM - Night Out

Cairo Jazz Club

Live music, good energy, and Cairo's creative crowd. This is where Saturday nights happen. Stay as late as you dare.

Live Music Nightlife

3 Sunday: The Recovery

11:00 AM - Slow Morning

Zooba, Zamalek

Egyptian comfort food for the soul. The ful and taameya will fix everything. Chase it with fresh mango juice.

Casual Recovery
1:00 PM - Final Stroll

Zamalek Wander

One last walk through the tree-lined streets. Pick up some souvenirs, grab a coffee, and say goodbye (for now) to Cairo.

Walking Shopping
