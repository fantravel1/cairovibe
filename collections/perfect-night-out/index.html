The Perfect Cairo Night Out

🕐 6 PM - 6 AM 📍 6 Stops 💰 $100-200 Budget

Twelve hours of Cairo magic. We've mapped out the perfect evening from golden hour rooftop drinks to dancing under the stars - and the late-night street food that follows. This is how locals do it.

1
6:00 PM - SUNSET

The Roof at Kempinski

Start with the best view in Cairo. Arrive by 6 PM to catch the golden hour as the sun sets over the Nile. Order their signature cocktails and watch the city transform as the lights come on.

📍 Garden City 💰 $$$ 🍸 2-3 drinks
2
8:00 PM - DINNER

Sequoia

Walk over to Zamalek for dinner at Cairo's most scenic restaurant. The Nile-side terrace is magical at night. Go for the grilled seafood and mezze - shareable and perfect for groups.

📍 Zamalek 💰 $$$ ⏱ 1.5-2 hours
3
10:30 PM - PRE-GAME

The Garden

A short walk to this hidden gem for after-dinner cocktails. The garden setting is intimate and the cocktail menu is creative. This is where the night really starts.

📍 Zamalek 💰 $$ 🍸 2 drinks
4
12:00 AM - LIVE MUSIC

Cairo Jazz Club

The main event. Cairo's legendary live music venue is just hitting its stride at midnight. Check their schedule for the night - could be jazz, funk, or electronic depending on the day.

📍 Agouza 💰 $$ ⏱ 2-3 hours
5
3:00 AM - LATE NIGHT

After Hours Bar

If you're still going strong, the night doesn't end. Several spots in Zamalek and Downtown keep pouring until dawn. Ask the locals at CJC where the after-party is.

📍 Various 💰 $$ 🌙 Until dawn
6
5:00 AM - RECOVERY

Late Night Koshari

End the night right with Egypt's ultimate comfort food. Downtown's 24-hour koshari spots are a rite of passage. Carbs, spice, and the satisfaction of a night well lived.

📍 Downtown 💰 $ 🍴 Perfect ending

Pro Tips

  • Book The Roof and Sequoia in advance - especially on weekends
  • Cairo Jazz Club can get crowded - arrive before midnight for good spots
  • Uber/Careem is your friend - don't drive after drinking
  • Dress smart casual - no shorts or sandals at nicer venues
  • Thursday and Friday nights are the biggest nights out
  • Budget around 2000-4000 EGP ($65-130) for the full experience

What to Wear

Smart casual is the sweet spot. Men: collared shirt, nice jeans or chinos, closed shoes. Women: cocktail dress or stylish separates. The rooftops and Cairo Jazz Club have relaxed dress codes but you want to look good. Avoid sportswear and sneakers at upscale venues.
