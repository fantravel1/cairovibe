Kempinski Nile Hotel

★ 4.6 $$$$ 📍 Garden City 🏨 191 Rooms
Nile Views Boutique Fine Dining Spa

European refinement on the Nile. The Kempinski offers a more intimate alternative to Cairo's mega-hotels. With fewer than 200 rooms, personalized service, and the acclaimed Osmanly restaurant, it's a favorite of discerning travelers who appreciate understated luxury.

The Rooms

Contemporary elegance with Art Deco touches, many rooms featuring direct Nile views. The attention to detail is evident - from the Italian linens to the marble bathrooms to the intuitive technology. Suites offer expansive living spaces and butler service.

Room Categories

Deluxe Room (City View)From $280

Dining

Osmanly is the hotel's crown jewel - one of Cairo's finest restaurants, serving refined Ottoman and Turkish cuisine. Jazz Bar offers cocktails with live music, while Chocolate Lounge is perfect for afternoon tea.

Pro Tips

  • Book Nile-view rooms - the sunrise is spectacular
  • Osmanly requires reservations, even for hotel guests
  • The rooftop pool is smaller but beautifully designed
  • Walking distance to Tahrir Square and Egyptian Museum
