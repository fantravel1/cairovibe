French luxury meets Cairo charm at the southern tip of Gezira Island. The Sofitel combines an unbeatable Zamalek location with panoramic Nile views, world-class dining, and that distinctly French approach to hospitality.

View Rooms Dining

The Hotel

Rising 27 floors above the Nile, the Sofitel occupies one of Cairo's prime locations. You're in the heart of Zamalek - steps from the neighborhood's best restaurants and bars - yet the hotel feels like a private retreat. The views from higher floors are spectacular: the river below, Downtown Cairo across the water, and the Pyramids visible on clear days.

The French heritage shows in every detail: the elegant lobby, the attentive service, the quality of the linens. It's luxury without stuffiness, sophisticated but welcoming.
