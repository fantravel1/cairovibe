Marriott Mena House

★ 4.8 $$$$ 📍 Giza 🏛 Pyramid Views
Historic Pool Gardens Once in a Lifetime

There is no hotel in the world like Mena House. Originally a 19th-century hunting lodge, this palace hotel sits at the very foot of the Pyramids. Open your curtains to the Great Pyramid. Have breakfast with the Sphinx. This is bucket-list hospitality.

Check Availability Dining

The Experience

Mena House has hosted royalty, world leaders, and celebrities for over 150 years. Churchill and Roosevelt met here during WWII. Agatha Christie wrote here. Every guest becomes part of this extraordinary history.

The hotel spreads across 40 acres of manicured gardens that border the Pyramid complex. You can literally walk to the Pyramids from the property. Rooms in the historic Palace Wing feature antique furniture and traditional Egyptian decor; the Garden Wing offers contemporary luxury. Both have views that will take your breath away.
