The St. Regis Cairo

★ 4.7 $$$$ 📍 Nile Corniche 🏨 286 Rooms
Butler Service Nile Views Spa Fine Dining

The St. Regis tradition of refined luxury comes to Cairo. Every guest receives personal butler service, rooms are among the largest in the city, and the attention to detail is exceptional. This is where discerning travelers stay when only the best will do.

The St. Regis Experience

Butler service isn't just a perk here - it's central to the St. Regis philosophy. Your butler handles everything from unpacking to reservations to that 3 AM craving. The rooms blend contemporary luxury with Egyptian influences, and those Nile views are truly spectacular.

Dining & Bars

J&G Steakhouse by Jean-Georges Vongerichten serves exceptional cuts. Tianma offers Pan-Asian cuisine. The St. Regis Bar continues the brand's tradition of sophisticated cocktail culture.

Pro Tips

  • The Bloody Mary ritual at St. Regis Bar is worth experiencing
  • Book the Iridium Spa for their signature hammam treatment
  • Nile-view suites have the most spectacular sunsets
  • The breakfast buffet is one of Cairo's finest
