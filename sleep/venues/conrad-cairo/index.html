The Conrad Cairo delivers polished international luxury right on the Nile. The Hotel

Rising 24 floors above the Nile Corniche, the Conrad Cairo has been a fixture of Cairo's luxury hotel scene since 2003. The striking contemporary tower offers commanding views of the river and city from its higher floors, while the ground level connects directly to the World Trade Center, making it ideal for business travelers.

The interiors blend modern elegance with subtle Egyptian touches - geometric patterns, warm colors, and occasional artifacts that nod to the country's heritage without becoming themed. The overall feel is sophisticated and international, with service standards you'd expect from the Hilton luxury tier.
