Cairo's Best Rooftop Bars

Drinks with a view. Cairo's chaotic beauty is best appreciated from above - here's where to sip cocktails with the city sprawling beneath you.

Cairo rewards those who go high. From the ribbons of the Nile to the distant pyramids to the forest of minarets and satellite dishes, the city's rooftops offer perspective in every sense. Here are the best places to drink in the view.

The Roof - Four Seasons Nile Plaza

Garden City | $$$$

Cairo's most glamorous rooftop. The Four Seasons delivers a polished experience - craft cocktails, an extensive wine list, and unobstructed Nile views. The crowd is well-heeled, the dress code is smart casual, and the sunset hour is magical.

Best for: Special occasions, impressive dates, elegant evenings

Cairo Tower Revolving Restaurant

Zamalek | $$

Yes, it's touristy. Yes, the revolving floor is a bit kitschy. But 187 meters up, with 360-degree views of the entire city? It's an experience. Go for sunset drinks and watch Cairo transform from dusty afternoon to glittering night.

Best for: First-time visitors, 360-degree panoramas, unique experience

Crimson Bar - Conrad Cairo

Corniche | $$$

A newer addition to the scene with a focus on craft cocktails. The design is contemporary and striking, the bartenders know their stuff, and the Nile glitters below. Popular with Cairo's young professionals.

Best for: Cocktail enthusiasts, after-work drinks, Nile views

Pier 88

Zamalek | $$$

Technically on the Nile rather than above it, but the terrace views are spectacular. Pier 88 combines excellent seafood with a killer drink menu. The setting sun over the water is reason enough to visit.

Best for: Seafood + drinks, waterfront atmosphere, group outings

Bab El Sharq Terrace

Heliopolis | $$

Not the highest, but the rooftop terrace of this traditional restaurant offers a different vibe - lanterns, cushions, live music drifting up from below. Pair shisha with mezze and watch Heliopolis's heritage buildings glow.

Best for: Traditional atmosphere, shisha, live music nights

💡 Rooftop Tips

  • Sunset is prime time everywhere - arrive early on weekends
  • Smart casual dress code at hotel rooftops
  • Winter evenings can be chilly - bring a layer
  • Reservations recommended Thursday-Saturday
