Maadi for Families

The expat-friendly suburb that's perfect for families.

Maadi's tree-lined streets, international schools, and family-friendly venues make it Cairo's premier family neighborhood. Here's your guide.

Kid-Friendly Eats

The Smokery - BBQ, high chairs, messy-friendly
Lucille's - American diner vibes, kids menu
Beano's - Spacious cafe with room for strollers

Parks & Green Spaces

Wadi Degla Protectorate offers hiking trails, and various clubs have playgrounds and pools. The streets themselves are walker-friendly compared to most of Cairo.

Parents Night Out

When you need grown-up time: Tempo wine bar, The Tap for craft beers, and the various Road 9 restaurants offer sophisticated dining within walking distance.
