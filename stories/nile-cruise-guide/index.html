Nile Dinner Cruise Guide

Dining on the river. From feluccas to luxury cruisers.

There's something magical about dinner on the Nile - Cairo's skyline glittering past as you float on waters that have sustained civilizations. Here's how to do it.

Luxury Dinner Cruises

Le Pacha 1901, Nile Maxim, and other large cruise boats offer buffet dinners with entertainment. Best for tourists and special occasions. Book ahead.

Budget: EGP 800-1,500/person

Private Felucca

Hire a traditional sailboat for a sunset cruise. Bring your own picnic and drinks. Romantic and budget-friendly. Depart from Zamalek or Maadi.

Budget: EGP 200-400/hour for the boat

Floating Restaurants

Blue Nile Boat, Nile City Boats, and others stay moored but still deliver the river experience. Good food without the touristy shows.

Budget: EGP 400-800/person
