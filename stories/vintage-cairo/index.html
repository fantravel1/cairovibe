Vintage Cairo

Historic venues where Cairo's past lives on.

Cairo has layers. Beneath the modern city lies another - one of belle époque elegance, Ottoman grandeur, and mid-century glamour. These venues let you taste it.

El Fishawi

Khan el-Khalili's legendary coffee house, serving since the 1700s. Mirrors, brass, and the ghosts of Nobel laureates past.

Groppi

Once Cairo's most glamorous tearoom. The Art Deco interiors remain, echoing a more elegant era.

Abou Shakra

Four generations of Egyptian grills since 1947. The lamb is legendary, the tradition unbroken.

Cap D'Or

Downtown's last Greek bar, unchanged since mid-century. Ouzo, old men, and authentic atmosphere.
