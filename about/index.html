About CairoVibe

Cairo After Dark. Cairo Alive.

The Ultimate Guide to Going Out in Cairo

CairoVibe is a global, multilingual platform capturing the real, electric, modern vibe of Cairo. We're built for young Egyptians, global travelers, creatives, night owls, and anyone who wants to experience Cairo now, not just read about its past.

This is not a museum guide. It's a living city energy map.

What We Cover

  • Best places to go out - clubs, bars, rooftops, live music
  • Where young people actually eat - street food to cool spots
  • Where to sleep across all price points
  • Cafes, hangouts, and third places
  • Culture, art, and underground scenes
  • How Cairo feels at night vs day

"No tourist traps. No outdated cliches. No unsafe advice."

Our Philosophy

CairoVibe is youth-centered but not exclusive. We're culture-aware without being preachy. Every recommendation is evidence-based - we verify, we visit, we update.

We believe Cairo is one of the world's great cities - vibrant, chaotic, beautiful, and endlessly surprising. Our mission is to help you experience the best of it.

How We Work

Every listing on CairoVibe is verified through our evidence system:

Tier A - Firsthand local verification
Tier B - Reputable local media sources
Tier C - Verified traveler reports
Tier D - Never published (unverified)
